1 Cookies

1.1 What are cookies?

Cookies are a technology that allows the site to store some data on yout browser.

1.2 How do we use cookies?

We use cookies to enhance your user experience on our site. Soon we will implement functionalities that allow you to save some dice combinations. These will be saved as cookies. We also use cookies for Google Analytics (see below).

2 Google Analytics

2.1 What is Google Analytics?

Google Analytics, if used, gives the site authors metrics on site use (how many people use the site, how often, at what time, ...)

2.2 How do we use Google Analytics?

If allowed, we collect only the basic data on your use of the site.

  • We neither collect nor process PII (Personally Identifiable Information).
